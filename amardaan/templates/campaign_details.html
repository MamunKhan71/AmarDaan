Campaign Story

In the quiet town of Hopeville, a heart beats with boundless dreams - Ratan's. At just 10, he was diagnosed with a rare heart condition, but his spirit soared higher than any ailment could reach. Every day, he'd gaze at the horizon, longing to run like the wind.

"Run for Ratan" is more than a campaign; it's a race against time, a sprint towards hope. We envision a world where Ratan's laughter fills the air, where his tiny feet dance with abandon. With your support, we aim to give him the gift of a new heart and a chance to chase his dreams.

Together, let's turn every heartbeat into a promise. Every dollar brings us closer to the finish line. Join us in this journey of love, hope, and unwavering determination. Run alongside Ratan as he races towards a future full of possibilities.

Donate today and let's make Ratan's dreams take flight. Every step counts. Every heartbeat matters. Run for Ratan.
